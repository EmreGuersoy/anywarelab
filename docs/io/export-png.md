# Export PNG

Click **Export ▾ → PNG image** to download a clean raster image of your labware design.

## What is included

- White background with padding
- Plate outline (white fill, dark border)
- All wells with their labels (A1, B1, …)

## What is excluded

- Background dot grid
- Dimension annotations and axis labels
- Fiducial markers
- Selection highlights and violation rings
- Canvas UI chrome

## Resolution

The image is rendered at **6 px/mm**. A standard SBS plate (127.76 × 85.48 mm) exports at
approximately **810 × 560 px** — sharp enough for documentation or lab notes.

## Filename

The PNG is named after the **Load name** field (e.g. `custom_labware_1.png`).
